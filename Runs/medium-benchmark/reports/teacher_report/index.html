About this table
What it shows: Each row is a teacher model. Columns include discrimination metrics (V1/S2/R3) measuring how well the teacher's items spread students apart, plus the count of generated datapoints.
V1 (Variance) — variance of per-student average scores. Higher = teacher items discriminate better between strong and weak students.
S2 (Spread) — mean absolute deviation of per-student averages. Robust alternative.
R3 (Range) — max minus min per-student average. Simple spread measure.
⚠ST flags self-teaching (teacher = student) — scores may be inflated.
