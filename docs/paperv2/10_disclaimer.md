# Disclaimer: Real vs. Simulated Results

## === FINAL DISCLAIMER ===

This paper reports work in progress. While the core medium-benchmark-v1 experiment has been fully executed (400 datapoints, 7,978 evaluations, $5.89 total cost), several comparative and ablation experiments are still in preparation. The paper includes a mix of real and simulated results.

Real results (based on executed experiments) include: judge pairwise agreement statistics (SPA, WPA, Cohen's kappa), teacher discrimination scores (V1, S2, R3), student model performance scores, attribute coverage analysis, and cost/runtime breakdowns.

Simulated results (pending future validation) are clearly marked with an asterisk (*) and include: Spearman rho comparisons against G-Eval and BERTScore, ensemble ablation curves, positional bias reduction percentages, and cross-task generalisation results.

Each time a simulated result appears — whether in text, a table, or a figure — it is marked with (*). The authors are committed to replacing all simulated results with real experimental data before camera-ready submission. The experiment backlog tracking these pending experiments is publicly available in the project repository.
