Output format: A single sentence (20-50 words) summarising the most important fact or event described in the article. No bullet points. No preamble.

Target Attributes

complexitysimple, moderate, complex, technical
domainscience, business, politics, technology, health, other

Nuanced Attributes

article_lengthshort_200w, medium_400w, long_600w
narrative_typeevent_report, feature_piece, analysis_commentary

Sampling: 100 items  |  target attrs: [1]  |  nuance attrs: [1, 2]

Rubric (3 criteria)

faithfulness[BA] The summary contains no hallucinated or incorrect facts relative to the source article. High = fully faithful; Medium = minor omission or slight imprecision; Low = factual error or unsupported claim.
information_coverageThe summary captures the single most important fact or event from the article. High = central point clearly conveyed; Medium = main point present but incomplete; Low = peripheral or wrong focus.
concisenessThe summary is appropriately brief (one sentence, ≤50 words) with no redundancy or filler phrases. High = tight and precise; Medium = slightly wordy; Low = multi-sentence or padded.
